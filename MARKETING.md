- **Does not contain:** Full product emotional loop detail, Insight schema/prompt rules, or unproven performance declarations presented as fact.
- **Update when:** Audience framing, claims ladder, or proof requirements change with acceptance.
- **Default loading behavior:** Load for marketing, public language, claims, or competitive positioning work.

---

## Target audience

Young adult women in the United States and other higher-purchasing-power markets, framed respectfully around journaling, interpretation, and self-reflection.

Product owner detail: [PRODUCT.md](./PRODUCT.md).

---

## General-model substitution problem

A user can open a general frontier model, create a dream journal, and request interpretations.

DreamCatcher must win on lower recurring effort, specialized Insight quality, longitudinal continuity, Sheepy, emotional reward, and repeat-use ritual.

---

## Acquisition vs retention devices

- Desktop/web may be stronger for beautiful acquisition and Reddit marketing.
- Mobile is stronger for retention (capture in bed / after waking).
- Desktop can drive acquisition; mobile must drive retention; mobile cannot feel inferior.

---

## Sheepy’s emotional role

Sheepy is the emotional companion and central product identity.

- Emotion is the hook.
- Interpretation depth is the substance.
- Never accept an Insight regression merely to improve emotional presentation.

---

## Interpretation vs reflection

- Interpretation remains the stronger consumer category and promise.
- Reflection describes the responsible method.
- Interpretations must not be presented as diagnostic or scientifically certain.

## Claims ladder

| Level | Claim | Status |
|-------|-------|--------|
| Accepted internal description | “Sheepy is a specialized dream-intelligence and interpretation system built around a frontier model, with DreamCatcher’s constraints, context, schema, continuity, evaluation harness, persistence, and emotional experience.” | Accepted for internal use |
| Reasonable consumer description | “Sheepy is DreamCatcher’s specialized dream AI.” | Reasonable if not overextended |
| Testable hypothesis | DreamCatcher can provide better dream interpretation with less prompting effort than general frontier models and major dream apps. | Hypothesis — not a public proof point yet |
| Unproven claim | “Sheepy is the best dream-interpretation model.” | Not allowed as established fact |
| Prohibited without evidence | DreamCatcher trained a proprietary foundation model, or provides scientifically authoritative / diagnostic dream conclusions | Prohibited |

Do not turn marketing ambition into established performance claims.

## Future blind benchmark

Compare Sheepy with:

1. strongest relevant frontier models
2. an ordinary realistic user prompt
3. an expert purpose-built prompt
4. major dream-interpretation competitors

Use the same dream and waking context.

Score:

- meaningful self-understanding
- unsupported inference
- repetition
- emotional resonance
- usefulness
- constructive pushback
- desire to return

Do not assume Sheepy wins. Consistent blind wins could become a major marketing proof point.
